NFC Cards with RFID Module

When the user presses the "save" button, the music will be saved to an NFC card in
an instant by the RFID module in the device, which can also read the card to
playback the music without delay. The data stored in the card takes the format of
the song ID, matching the music file from the database.

Plus, the NFC card is compatible with almost all the mainstream mobile phones,
making it possible for the younger generations to play the in-card music with their
phones, a way to facilitate communication among the generations.

The music in a card is not able to be rewritten due to the consideration of
the elderly being more prone to mistake, also because the song relates to a special
memory of them, which they do not wish to change or wipe away.

The Welcome Kit

A welcome kit includes three pre-recorded cards that contain a short audio
introduction of the device and another ten blank cards, and it comes along in the
package to help the elderly users get onboard. More music cards can be ordered at
the price of 3€ for ten pieces.

Music Switch

Early research shows that the elderly strongly prefer the music of their young adult
years and that music and reminiscence are valuable for improving their wellbeings.
Therefore, more delicate and personal approaches to choosing music are
adopted: a slider corresponding to the timeline of their lives, and four thematics
evoking the rich emotions from the past, both being expressed through the music.

Internet Access

With the partnership with Spotify, the device can access the numerous music
resource online and sort them accordingly. Using the Spotify API it can also learn
from the user's preferences of the saved songs to generate a personalized playlist.
