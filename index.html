Tangible music for tangible memories to share

“How might we help the elderly enjoy old music that they can relate to?”
The
Context

Nowadays, the elderly are increasingly forced to interface themselves with new technologies: these technologies are very distant from their way of thinking and interacting with the world; meanwhile, they are more used to interface themselves with physical objects and tangible concepts.


If only they had a chance to understand and use them easily, these technologies could improve the elderly’s daily activities helping them in facing difficulties and relieving the sense of loneliness which often pervades their days making them feel distant and isolated from the world.
